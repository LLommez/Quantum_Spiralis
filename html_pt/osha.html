Harmonic Oscillator

The harmonic oscillator is a fundamental model in quantum mechanics. It describes a particle subject to a restoring force proportional to its displacement, commonly represented by a spring or a pendulum.

Energy Levels

The energy of a quantum harmonic oscillator is quantized. The energy levels are given by the formula:

Energy (En) = (n + 1/2)ħω

where:

  • n is the quantum number (0, 1, 2...)
  • ħ is the reduced Planck's constant
  • ω is the angular frequency
